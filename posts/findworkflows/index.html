Find Workflow Associations and History Lists

Jul 21, 2020 • edited Jul 22, 2020

Find all 2010 and 2013 Workflows, their associated history lists and running instances

As SharePoint 2010 Workflows are sunsetting in SharePoint Online SharePoint 2010 Workflow Retirement, it becomes even more important to inventory, rework and/or dispose of SharePoint 2010 workflows in your on premises SharePoint Farms. To that end, I have written a PowerShell script to find all workflows (2010 or 2013) and record the interesting details about the workflow in a CSV file. This script and resulting inventory can also be quite helpful when cleaning up before an upgrade or due to content DB bloat (think Workflow History Lists running wild).

Script available here - FindWorkflowsAndHistoryLists.ps1
